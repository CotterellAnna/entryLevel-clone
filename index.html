Mentored Designed Experiential Learning

Each experience has a committee of experienced professionals who design the content and assessments you will engage in.

Each experience has a lead mentor who manages the overall flow of the course.

You’ll work alongside a team of 20-30 other students from around the world. You’ll all develop your skills together, exchanging ideas, solutions and encouragement.

Get feedback, ask questions, and learn from others. You are not alone.

The Commitment Bond

Let's make a commitment to learn. Together.

Pay the $99 Bond
Instead of thousands of dollars, our base price is low and we have financial aid for those who need it.
Finish the coursework in 6 weeks
No pass/fail grades. As long as you do the course work to a minimum standard (no copying other people and some attempt at the questions) you'll qualify.
Get a Refund or Opt-in for Premium Perks
Once you’ve finished the course on time you can get all your money back or you can opt-in for some premium perks.

What if I don't finish?

Congratulations! You've bought a course

Most companies charge you thousands of dollars to help you have “skin in the game”. While its true that financial cost can be an incentive, if that's all they cared about they would refund you when you finish.

If you don’t finish, you retain access to the content. (People who opt for a refund lose access immediately after the course is over) In essence, you have nothing to lose because the worst case is you’ve paid $99 for a course. Which is what most companies do anyway. We just want to provide a way to incentivise you to get to the finish line.

EntryLevel makes money in a number of ways:
  • Premium perks are offered upon completion of the program, such as premium certificates, bonus content and other perks.
  • Commitment Bonds that are forfeited when people don't finish
  • Enterprise/Business recruitment fees

You can still enter the course!

We offer financial aid to those who are unable to pay the commitment bond.

To apply for financial aid, follow the steps below:

  • Create an EntryLevel account - this step is completely free.
  • When you log in to your account, you can select the course to sign up to.
  • You will be taken to a payment page. On this page you will be given the option to apply for financial aid.
Financial aid is granted based on your application. We also have a Referral Scheme!

At the end of the program (if you graduate) you are offered the Premium Perks, which include:
  • A premium certificate
  • Continued access to the course content
  • Bonus content and resources based on the program you completed

To purchase these perks all you have to do is let us keep your commitment bond! Please note that the Premium Perks are completely optional.

Users who claim their refund will still receive a standard certificate of completion and will have their access to the content revoked after the program ends.
